         Name: SpritesRTS
       Status: Windows(Y), Linux(Y)
        Level: Intermediate
   Maintainer: Kenneth (sphair)
      Summary: Demonstrates sprites in a simple RTS engine

Left click on unit to select
Left click and drag mouse to select area
Hold shift to add to selection
Left click on nothing to deselect

Right click on landscape to move
Right click on other unit to attack
Shift right click to force attack
Ctrl and shift to fire nuke

Escape to quit
